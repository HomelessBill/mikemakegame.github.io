About Mike

I've spent the last 15 years in the games industry being a jack of all trades and working with tons of great teams from Firaxis to Gearbox. I've played nearly every class in the games industry, and I enjoyed all of it. After spending a lot of my recent years as tank, healer, and support, I'm jonesing to play some DPS again. I helped everyone else ship their broken messes, and now I'm excited to make my own.

I cut my teeth at Mass Media and learned from 30-year industry veterans, working my way up from baby Junior Engineer to baby Lead Engineer. After getting acquired by 2K I became an Engineering Director and Helldiver in Chief, tackling strategic planning, interdepartmental communication, systems design, and - when things got too fun - heavy-hitting, hands-on engineering into the night.
